What is Elbeds?

Elbeds is an online platform for users who are looking for a place to live and owners who are offering their properties for rent. It's main purpose is to help students to find their ideal home around the university given their budget constraint and preferred location.

Features

Easy search box

An easy to use search box on the homepage. Just enter your budget range and preferred location, and you are done.

Reviews and Comments

Elbeds can help you to easily know which places should you avoid or choose based from the experiences of others.

Wide Selection

Elbeds shows many different kinds of place to live -- dorms, apartments, bedspace, hotels, motels and resorts.

Google Maps Integration

You can easily locate and see what are the nearby landmarks and restaurants of your preferred place.

Free Listings

If you are an owner, you can freely enlist your properties with descriptions, photographs, price and address.

Customizable Searches

You can filter your searches in terms of location, price, type of property, age of the property, and amenities.
